      Quick Reference

      Help

      Welcome Adventurer!

      Click on the name to change it to your personal name. If you are fortunate enough to survive into the depths, you may be able to save your high score for all to revere at your amazing abilities!

      Goal

      Explore each floor, gather stronger gear, survive the denizens of Hallowdeep, and descend when you find the stairs. Your run score grows from XP, kills, depth, and level.

      Mouse And Touch

      Hover or tap the dungeon to examine a tile. On small screens, use the direction buttons below the map and the inventory buttons for waiting or drinking tonics.

      Tips

      • Moving into a monster attacks it; monsters act after your turn.
      • When you find equipment, choose whether to equip it, pack it, or leave it.
      • Status effects fade over turns, but waiting still gives nearby monsters a chance to act.
      • Use the examine panel to check monster abilities before committing to a fight.
      • Watch your HP! Drink a pumpkin tonic so you last longer.
